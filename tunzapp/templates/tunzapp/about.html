About Us

We have noticed that in our society, there are so many cases of children who are bright, focused and have big dreams, but most of these dreams end up being abandoned simply because these children come from struggling families. Unless their stories are aired on tv’s, it’s almost impossible to know if they even exists or if their stories are actually true.

For a fact we know that there are so many well wishers out there who are willing to offer support to these children but knowing who they are or how to reach to them and support them is difficult or even impossible, unless the media airs their stories. So it is for this reason that we thought of creating a web application to ensure not only the NGO’S but also individual well wishers get an opportunity to create an impact to these children’s lives and ensure they get to live up to their full potential in life. Lea mtoto is a platform that brings together donors to support needy students across Kenya. The platform lists a record of verified needy students, their bio, areas of needs, academic achiements etc.
