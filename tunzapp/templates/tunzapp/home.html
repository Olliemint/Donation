How This All Works

STEP 1

Is to identify the child who is need of financial support. Analyze the specific needs of that particular child as different children have different needs eg. money for upkeep,clothes,shoes,books

STEP 2

We then do a background check of the child to eliminate imposters who dont require financial support. We do this by visiting the childs home, meeting and interviewing the parents,check on the financial status of the family as a whole.

STEP 2

After verification we then proceed to register the child in the system ,so that the donors can view them in the website and contribute towards their needs.
